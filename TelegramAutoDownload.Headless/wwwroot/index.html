How monitoring works

☑ Monitor — forward-only: captures new messages as they arrive. Does not scan old history. Uncheck to stop queue processing for that chat.
Bootstrap — rate-limited history scan → items queued in the database → downloaded gradually (safe for huge chats).
Test 10 — download the newest 10 media items as a probe (no queue DB writes). Review the report before bootstrap.
Clear queue — on the Queue tab, removes all tracked items for a chat from the database (files on disk are kept).
